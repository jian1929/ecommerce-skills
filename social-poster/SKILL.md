# E-Commerce Social Media Poster

Generate engaging social media content for e-commerce brands.

## When to Use

Use this skill when the user wants to:
- Create social media posts for products
- Write promotional captions and ad copy
- Generate content calendars
- Create platform-specific content (Instagram, TikTok, Twitter, Facebook)
- Repurpose product descriptions for social channels

## Supported Platforms

- Instagram, TikTok, Twitter/X, Facebook, LinkedIn, Pinterest

## Workflow

### Step 1: Content Discovery
Gather information:
1. What is the product or promotion?
2. Target platform(s)
3. Content goal (awareness, sales, engagement)
4. Brand voice and key messages
5. Any specific offers or discounts?

### Step 2: Content Generation

**For Product Posts:**
1. Highlight key product benefits (1-3 points)
2. Include relevant hashtags (5-15 based on platform)
3. Add call-to-action
4. Consider platform-specific formats

**For Promotional Content:**
1. Lead with value (discount percentage, offer)
2. Create urgency (limited time, while supplies last)
3. Include social proof if available
4. Clear CTA to shop/purchase

**For Engagement Posts:**
1. Ask questions to drive comments
2. Share behind-the-scenes content
3. User-generated content repurposing
4. Brand story moments

### Step 3: Content Calendar

Generate a weekly/monthly posting schedule:

```markdown
## Content Calendar [Week of DATE]

### Monday - Product Feature
- Platform: Instagram
- Format: Carousel post
- Caption: [Draft]
- Hashtags: [List]
- Best time: 11 AM EST

### Wednesday - Engagement
- Platform: TikTok
- Format: Behind-the-scenes video
- Hook: [Attention-grabbing opening]
- CTA: Follow for more

### Friday - Promotion
- Platform: Facebook
- Format: Single image + link
- Offer: [Current promotion]
- Urgency: [Limited time language]
```

## Platform-Specific Guidelines

### Instagram
- Captions: 125-300 characters ideal, max 2,200
- Hashtags: 5-15 in first comment or caption end
- Hook: First 125 characters most important

### TikTok
- Captions: 100-150 characters
- Hook: First 3 seconds critical
- Hashtags: 3-5 trending + 1 niche

### Twitter/X
- Tweets: 200-280 characters
- Thread format for longer content
- Hashtags: 1-2 max

### Facebook
- Captions: 40-80 characters for max engagement
- Longer for link posts
- Emojis increase engagement

## Content Formulas

**AIDA for E-Commerce:**
```
Attention: [Eye-catching opening - question, stat, or bold statement]
Interest: [Product benefit or story]
Desire: [Social proof, scarcity, or emotional appeal]
Action: [Shop now, link in bio, limited time]
```

**PAS (Problem-Agitate-Solution):**
```
Problem: [Common pain point]
Agitate: [Expand on the problem]
Solution: [Your product as answer]
```

## Best Practices

1. **Platform-Optimize**: Adapt tone and format per platform
2. **Visual Guidance**: Include photo/video suggestions
3. **Track Performance**: Recommend metrics to monitor
4. **Batch Content**: Generate 5-10 posts at once when possible
5. **Seasonal Alignment**: Match content to holidays/events

## Example Prompts

- "Create a week's worth of Instagram posts for our new skincare line"
- "Write TikTok captions for product showcase videos"
- "Generate a content calendar for a 20% off sale"
- "Create promotional posts for our best-selling product"
- "Write Twitter/X threads announcing a new product launch"
